Events

The San Diego chapter of Pink Pistols offers various classes and events that teach self defense, both with and without firearms.

While we do not restrict anyone based on their LGBT status, please understand that anyone who comes to any of our events must be legally allowed to possess a firearm or ammunition, whether or not firearms are being used at that particular event. Information on who may or may not possess firearms or ammunition can be found here.

If you want to join us for any of our upcoming events, please RSVP to them on our Facebook page.
