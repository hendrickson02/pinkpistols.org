Events

The San Diego chapter of Pink Pistols offers various classes and events that teach self defense, both with and without firearms.

While we do not restrict anyone based on their LGBT status, please understand that anyone who comes to any of our events must be legally allowed to possess a firearm or ammunition, whether or not firearms are being used at that particular event. More information on this subject can be accessed here.

If you want to join us for any of our upcoming events, be sure to join our training group on Facebook.
